Hello! I'm Sara Mohsenpour, a passionate professional with a rich background in the publishing field and a keen eye for design. Over the years, I have developed my skills in graphic design, specializing in book covers and text layout, and have led significant projects in the publishing industry.
My Journey
While studying for my Bachelor in Agriculture Engineering at Urmia University, I worked part-time at Caravan Publishing House. This early experience immersed me in the world of publishing and set my career on a different path. Since then, I have worked on countless book projects, collaborating with talented writers and artists to bring their visions to life. My work has involved everything from designing captivating book covers to meticulous text layouts, ensuring each publication is a work of art.
In addition to my publishing work, I've partnered with various publishers, designing and editing over 500 publications and managing projects that have significantly impacted the literary community in Iran and beyond.
Expanding Horizons
In 2023, I started a Diploma in Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College, Ottawa, expanding my skills in digital media, web development, and UI/UX design. I’ve gained expertise in tools like Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe XD, and Visual Studio Code, completing projects such as website mockups, UI/UX research, and moodboards.
My Vision
With a background in publishing and new skills in interactive media, I strive to craft compelling stories and engaging digital experiences. By blending visual storytelling with digital media expertise, I aim to create impactful marketing content and enhance brand messaging. 
Thank you for visiting my portfolio—explore my work and see my passion for design and storytelling in action.
