Creative Brief for a Unique Catering Brand: 
This brief is tailored to meet the client's needs and responses to our questionnaire. They run a unique catering business, aiming to transition from home-based to a storefront in Ottawa's Elgin Street. Specializing in mood-based flavors for events, they want their brand to convey elegance, joy, deliciousness, and fun. 
The brief covers key strategies for achieving their goals, including messaging, perception, focus, tone, mandates, selling points, emotional benefits, creative elements, and a strategic plan. Visual preferences lean towards deep purple and lime green, with a simple, text-focused logo.
Targeting socially active 30-40-year-old females or couples in the business sector, the brief aims to boost brand awareness and introduce new flavors. The project involves creating branding materials like a logo, business cards, and an elegant website, due for completion by early December, ahead of a significant event on December 11.
