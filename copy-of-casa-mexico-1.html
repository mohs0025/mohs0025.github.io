About IMD Showcase
IMD Showcase is an annual project designed to present the portfolios and achievements of graduating students from the Interactive Media Design (IMD) program at Algonquin College. Each showcase offers a unique platform for graduates to display their creative and technical skills in web design, multimedia, and digital storytelling.
The Fall 2024 edition of IMD Showcase aims to highlight the talents of IMD graduates through a refreshed, engaging website that appeals to key audiences, including prospective employers, future students, and alumni. This project not only celebrates the hard work and accomplishments of the graduates but also provides valuable exposure and networking opportunities within the digital media industry.
Current Website: 
Problems and Needs
•    Lack of Personality: Minimalist design doesn’t reflect creativity.
•    Poor Usability: Not responsive or accessible.
•    Missed Focus: Homepage wastes space on a logo animation.
•    Outdated Identity: Needs rebranding with a new logo, name, and theme.
•    Underwhelming Portfolios: Student talent isn’t highlighted enough.
•    Recruiter Challenges: Not recruiter-friendly.
•    Missing Content: Lacks IMD program and faculty info.

Solutions
•    Rebrand: Create a new logo, name, and theme.
•    Redesign: Improve usability, responsiveness, and accessibility.
•    Showcase Talent: Focus on student portfolios to attract recruiters.
•    Add Content: Include IMD program and faculty information.
